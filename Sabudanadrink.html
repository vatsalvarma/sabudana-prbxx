What’s Special About This Drink?

Sabudana Drink is inspired by traditional recipes often enjoyed during fasting days or when the body needs light yet sustaining nourishment. Sabudana pearls are known for providing quick energy while remaining easy to digest.

Combined with the gentle flavor of cardamom and the cooling touch of sabja seeds, this drink feels both soothing and satisfying. Shake & Gulp Sabudana Drink turns this traditional comfort into a ready-to-enjoy bottle. Since the pearls and seeds naturally settle, it’s meant to be shaken well before drinking.

Health Benefits

  • Cooling and soothing for the body
  • A comforting, nourishing refreshment

A traditional drink that fuels you gently.

Who Should Drink This?

Sabudana Drink is enjoyable for everyone as a light, nourishing beverage.

It is especially helpful for people who experience:

  • Low energy during long days
  • Fatigue when meals are delayed
  • A need for gentle nourishment
  • Light hunger between meals
  • A desire for something filling yet easy to digest

A soothing way to restore energy without feeling heavy.

Emotional Connect

Sabudana has long been part of traditional comfort foods — especially during fasting, when the body needed gentle strength and warmth. It represents care, patience, and nourishment passed down through generations.

Shake & Gulp Sabudana Drink brings that same comfort into your modern routine — soft, familiar, and made to support you through the day.

How to Enjoy
